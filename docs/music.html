Musicolinguistics

This project is rather separate from the rest of my work. In collaboration with Sam Mukherji of the School of Music, I am applying parsing technology to scores, in order to recover harmonic and contrapuntal structure, in the sense of a hierarchical representation of a "roman-numeral" analysis.

  • [79] Adapting language parsing to automate score reduction (talk at UM, 2019 May 15)

This work is part of a larger project on data science for music, funded by the Michigan Institute for Data Science (MIDAS).
